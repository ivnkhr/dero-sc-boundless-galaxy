Boundless Galaxy - blockchain based trading card game

EXPLORE - endless galaxies and discover different planets and players
COLONIZE - free slots and expand your empire
ENCHANT - your planets to excellence and start redeeming real DERO rewards
TRADE - your planet collection with others

All DERO sent to this contract are going to the shared pool and
All EXCELENT card are eligable to redeem percentage (y%) from this pool (each x blocks)

2. Goto Boundless Galaxy dApp page and navigate to Settings tab
3. Without additional plugins you will use wallet with curl manualy, so only daemon connection is important
Enter your local daemon address or enter remotely hosted deamon address http://37.139.4.77:30306

Blockchain interaction will be manual, but dApp will provide a snippet for each action

Authorization onchain

Auth process is essential to unlock personalized features like viewing your empire, setting nickname, modifying planet name, enchant and trade.
All instructions are mentioned on Settings page.

Setting Your Personal Alias / Nickname onchain

Change Nick button is availiable on Home tab. This feature will allow to set your nick across all dApp components, chat, galaxy, cards.

Chatting offchain

Chat is avaliable for all users and even unauth. There 2 type of chats General (mostly for trading) on Home tab. And per Section (mostly for casual chatting).
Attention! all chat messages are stored on 3rd party AWS service and not stored on chain.

Refreshing Game Status

Refresh Game State
Refresh button presents in header of each section that requires data from blockchain.
Use it after your interactive txs are mined to see changes.



Galaxy Exploration

There are virtualy endless sectors in Galaxy exploration tab.
Initialy there are no information about sector inhabitancy and its a subject for exploration.
After exploring a new sector it will reflect sector data on galaxy map.

Sector Exploration

Each sector contain of 7 colonizable orbits, each orbit can be colonized once.
If you want to obtain sertain locked planet, you need to find a way to contact planet owner nad buy it from him.
Each sector also features a chat.

Sector Set Moto onchain

Each sector features a functionality to set a message in top right corner, wich can only be overwritten by paying a certain fee.

Planet Colonization onchain

Each free slot of a sector can be colonized and planet card will be generated.
This card belongs to colonizer and noone can take it from him unless he sets his card on sale.
Detailed description of generation will be provided further...



Your Posessions

In Posessions tab you can see your collection or collection of any known DERO address (other users).
In order to see personalaized options (change desc, enchant, sell) you need to be authorized.

Planet Cards

After slot is colonized a planet will be generated.
There are 4 main components to a card:
1. Name, Owner and Description
2. Rarity / Attributes (Fire, Air, Watter, Magma, Terra, Ice, Light, Mist, Darkness)
3. Excelence / Overall Card Power (Avarage sum of all attributes)
4. Controll buttons (only if you authorized)

Lets explain rarity more detailed.
If attribute more or equals to 95 it will be marked as absolute attribute and be colored and will have additional star icon.
Having 3 and more absolute attributes will turn your card into RARE
More than 6 - LEGENDARY
And all 9 - EPIC


If avarage sum of all attributes greater or equal 95 card will recieve EXCELENT status and youll be able to Redeem portion of contracts shared pool.
Improving planet attributes can be achieved using Enchant functionality (explained below).

Planet Description onchain

By default each planet will show generic information like population, mass and size, but you can write your own history using Change Description in galaxy or posessions tab.

Planet Enchantment onchain

By pressing Enchant your planet will be selected (lets name it A) and youll be able to select another planet (lets name it B). After transaction executed planet B will be permamentaly destroyed and all of its 9 attribues will be added to planet A with a rate of ({{rootApp.settings_laoded.variable_enchant_precent}}%).
For example planet A (Darkness attribute = 85) and B (Darkness attribute = 75).
After B is destroyed and burned, planet A will now have 85 + (65*20%) = 85 + 13 = 98 and planet A darkness attribute will become absolute.

Redeeming EXCELENT Rewards onchain

When avarage sum of atributes larger or equal to 95% card gains EXCELENT status.
Now you can press Redeem and opbain portion of shared pool each {{rootApp.settings_laoded.variable_redeem_precent}} blocks.

Reward formula is (shared pool / amount of excelent cards) / reward percentage
(({{rootApp.onChain_value(rootApp.settings_laoded.balance_shared_pool)}} dero) / ({{rootApp.settings_laoded.stats_excelent_cards}} cards)) / ({{rootApp.settings_laoded.variable_redeem_precent}} %) = {{rootApp.display_avarage_reward()}} dero

Planet Auction (Setting Your Planet On Sale) onchain

If you got lucky and have uniq planet atributes or colonized highly demandable sector, you can put your planet on sale.
Youll be able to put any price you want.
In order to remove card from sale, simply put 0 price.

Planet Auction (Buing Someone Elses Planet) onchain

If someone put card on sale, you can buit if from sector view or posessions view by putting dero address in input field.
